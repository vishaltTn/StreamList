# Architecture Decision Records (ADR)

## Purpose

ADRs capture **why** we made important technical choices so future contributors (and future us) can align without re-litigating old debates.

## How to record a decision

1. Add a new section below. Pick **newest first** or **oldest first** and stick with it.
2. Use the template fields: **Date**, **Decision**, **Context**, **Alternatives considered**, **Consequences**.
3. One ADR per decision; split unrelated choices into separate entries.

---

## Example ADR (template)

**Date:** YYYY-MM-DD

**Decision:** Short title of what was decided.

**Context:** What problem or forces led to this? What constraints mattered?

**Alternatives considered:** What else was evaluated, and why it was not chosen (brief).

**Consequences:** What we gain, what we accept as trade-offs, and any follow-up work.

---

Record real ADRs in this file using the same fields as the template. Remove the example section once you have at least one real ADR, or keep it as a quick reference.
